How Kit Works

Exceptional hires start with one conversation.

Kit is an AI hiring agent. You describe who you need. Kit delivers a sourcing plan and a screened shortlist. You do the interviews.

Speak to Kit
01 — Kit learns

Kit learns what exceptional looks like.

Kit asks what would change if this hire were exceptional. What outcomes matter. What the last great person in this kind of role actually did.

A 10-minute conversation where Kit draws out the signal that matters — the difference between “managed stakeholders” and “rebuilt the relationship with the board after a failed product launch.”

Sample hiring conversation

Kit
AI Hiring Agent
Tell me about the last person in a similar role who really impressed you. What did they actually do?
Sarah. She joined when our data platform was a mess. Within 3 months she’d rebuilt the pipeline and we went from daily outages to 99.9% uptime.
That’s the signal. Someone who can walk into complexity and create order. Let me build your sourcing plan around that.
02 — Kit builds

Kit builds your sourcing plan.

By the end of the conversation, Kit delivers six elements built on what you told Kit — ready to execute.

CV gap analysis

What this role actually requires vs what CVs will tell you. The gaps Kit will screen for.

Top performer profile

Based on what you described, the specific outcomes and behaviours that define outstanding performance in this role.

Screening criteria

The questions and signals Kit will use to evaluate every applicant against your top performer profile.

High-performing job advert

An advert written around outcomes, not requirements. Designed to attract the people who’d actually deliver.

Voice screening for every applicant

Every single applicant gets a voice conversation with Kit. Not a form. A real, adaptive screening.

Top performer alerts

When Kit finds someone who matches the top performer profile, you get an immediate alert with a detailed assessment.
