## Questions

**Q: What is Kit?**
A: Kit is an AI hiring agent. You have a conversation with Kit about who you need, Kit builds a hiring plan, writes and posts the job ad, screens every applicant by voice conversation, and surfaces the best matches. You do the interviews.

**Q: Is Kit really free?**
A: Your first role is completely free. Kit builds your plan, writes the ad, screens applicants, and surfaces rockstars. No credit card required. Paid plans add more active roles, headhunting outreach, and network intelligence.

**Q: How does the screening work?**
A: Every applicant has a voice conversation with Kit. Not a form, not a chatbot. An adaptive conversation based on the screening questions from your hiring plan. Kit assesses against your rockstar bar and provides detailed reports.

**Q: How does Kit work alongside recruiters and agencies?**
A: Kit puts hiring tools directly in the hands of hiring managers, just like Lovable puts creation tools in the hands of creators. For talent teams and agencies, Kit extends their reach by handling screening, network mapping, and shortlisting at scale. Everyone benefits.

**Q: What types of roles does Kit work for?**
A: Any professional role. From senior engineering to operations, marketing to finance, product to sales. Kit adapts its screening to the outcomes that matter for each specific role.

**Q: How long does it take to get results?**
A: Your hiring plan arrives within the hour. Kit starts posting and screening immediately. Most users see their first screened shortlist within 3 hours of their conversation with Kit.

**Q: What is the Contxt network?**
A: Contxt is the intelligence layer Kit draws on. It maps 20m+ UK professionals, their career paths, and connections. Kit uses this to identify headhunting targets and network referrals for your role.

**Q: Do I need to learn a dashboard?**
A: No. Kit communicates with you directly. There's no dashboard to learn, no software to configure. Kit handles everything and sends you what you need.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. All conversations and hiring data are encrypted. Kit never contacts anyone without your permission. We comply with GDPR and maintain strict data handling practices.

**Q: Can I use Kit for multiple roles?**
A: The free plan includes 1 active role. Starter (£99/mo) includes 3 active roles. Pro (£299/mo) offers unlimited roles. Enterprise plans are available for larger organisations.
