## Hero

**Eyebrow:** HOW KIT WORKS
**H1:** Exceptional hires start with one conversation.
**Subhead:** Kit is an AI hiring agent. You describe who you need. Kit delivers a sourcing plan and a screened shortlist. You do the interviews.

## 01 — Kit learns what exceptional looks like

Kit asks what would change if this hire were exceptional. What outcomes matter. What the last great person in this kind of role actually did.

A 10-minute conversation where Kit draws out the signal that matters — the difference between "managed stakeholders" and "rebuilt the relationship with the board after a failed product launch."

### Sample hiring conversation
- Kit: Tell me about the last person in a similar role who really impressed you. What did they actually do?
- User: Sarah. She joined when our data platform was a mess. Within 3 months she'd rebuilt the pipeline and we went from daily outages to 99.9% uptime.
- Kit: That's the signal. Someone who can walk into complexity and create order. Let me build your sourcing plan around that.

## 02 — Kit builds your sourcing plan

By the end of the conversation, Kit delivers six elements built on what you told Kit — ready to execute.

1. **CV gap analysis** — What this role actually requires vs what CVs will tell you. The gaps Kit will screen for.
2. **Top performer profile** — Based on what you described, the specific outcomes and behaviours that define outstanding performance in this role.
3. **Screening criteria** — The questions and signals Kit will use to evaluate every applicant against your top performer profile.
4. **High-performing job advert** — An advert written around outcomes, not requirements. Designed to attract the people who'd actually deliver.
5. **Voice screening for every applicant** — Every single applicant gets a voice conversation with Kit. Not a form. A real, adaptive screening.
6. **Top performer alerts** — When Kit finds someone who matches the top performer profile, you get an immediate alert with a detailed assessment.

## 03 — Kit executes. You interview the best people.

- **Attracts candidates** — Kit writes an advert designed to attract the right people and posts it to premium job boards to maximise reach.
- **Screens every applicant** — Every single applicant gets a voice conversation with Kit. Not a form. A real, adaptive screening.
- **Alerts you to top performers** — When Kit finds someone who matches the top performer profile, you get an immediate alert with a detailed assessment.
- **Iterates with you** — Kit refines the search based on your feedback on each shortlisted candidate. It gets sharper over time.
