Common questions

Is the Free plan really free?

Yes. Your first role is completely free. Kit builds your sourcing plan, writes the ad, screens applicants, and alerts you to top performers. No credit card, no time limit on your first role.

What does Kit actually do?

Kit is an AI agent that handles everything: building your sourcing plan, writing and posting job adverts, screening every applicant by voice conversation, and alerting you when someone matches the top performer profile.

How does Kit work alongside recruiters and agencies?

Kit handles the heavy lifting of screening, planning and outreach, freeing up your team or agency partners to focus on relationship-building and closing. It extends everyone’s reach.

Can I cancel or pause anytime?

Yes. No lock-in contracts. Pause or cancel your subscription at any time. Active roles will continue until completed.

What types of roles does Kit work for?

Kit works for any professional role. From senior engineering to operations, marketing to finance. Kit adapts its screening to the outcomes that matter for each specific role.

What’s the difference between Growth and Enterprise?

Growth is the complete self-serve experience for hiring managers. Enterprise is for talent teams who need Kit embedded in their existing workflow — ATS integration, career site integration, multi-user management, and board contract integration.
